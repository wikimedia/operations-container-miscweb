Ready to get involved?

There are many many ways to get involved with Wikimedia—and all ways help our project grow. Everyday, volunteers around the world take on these tasks to make sure we can further expand free access to knowledge for every single human being.

You can:

Make a donation.
Create an account on Wikipedia to try new features, customize your experience, and contribute content.
Upload a photo to Wikimedia Commons.
Make your first simple edit to Wikipedia—like a spelling, or grammar issue.
Log some bugs! If you find bugs with the MediaWiki software we use to run Wikipedia and other Wikimedia Foundation projects, you can log a bug in the bug tracking system Phabricator.
If you are a developer, look for bugs in Phabricator you can help fix.
Adapted from Jared Zimmerman on Quora
