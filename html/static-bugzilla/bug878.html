Comment 14 Timeshifter 2013-06-17 04:37:08 UTC 
I removed the URL in the description because it does not illustrate anything. 

I had no idea until today that a list of templates used in a section existed. I knew only of the full page list. I did not know that a preview of a section edit would show a list of templates used in the section.

It is important to fix this because with the roll-out of the VisualEditor (VE) there is a huge necessity to simplify finding the stuff that VE can't edit. 

VE will more than likely have "edit source" links or icons for every section. People will be using those links all the time in order to try to edit or move around those templates in article sections. 

Transcluded templates in those sections will not be easily accessible to many editors due to the common lack of knowledge about how to get to them. I have tens of thousands of edits on Wikipedia and always went to the full-page list to find them. That is difficult. 

Concerning editing after VE is implemented there are multiple bug requests for ease of access to wikitext source editing of sections and templates in sections. See these discussions (or find them in the archives):
[[Wikipedia:VisualEditor/Feedback#Impossible_to_edit_sections_with_wikitext]]
[[Wikipedia:VisualEditor/Feedback#Now_unable_to_edit_sections_by_old_method]]

See 
* Bug 49665 - VisualEditor: Provide a get-me-into-the-wikitext-editor link on every header.
* Bug 49666 - VisualEditor: Display both [edit] and [edit source] on every header.
* Bug 48429 - VisualEditor: Support editing of sections inside a page.

Comment 16 Timeshifter 2013-06-18 09:37:48 UTC 
I discuss this further here:
[[Wikipedia:VisualEditor/Feedback]] - see the thread titled "View list of transcluded templates in a section" or something similar. You can find it in the archives when it is moved there. 

From what I can tell the visual editor edits template text poorly, and does not edit the formatting at all. I am not sure if VE can, or will be able to, edit transcluded templates at all. At least not where the transcluded template is used in a section of an article. In any case the most thorough editing of a template is done with the source editor. 

So we need quicker access to a list of transcluded templates used in that section of the article. Also, the name needs to be changed from "View templates on this page" to "View templates in this section".

[[Wikipedia:VisualEditor/Feedback]]. To see what I am talking about hit the edit source link for any discussion section with a Bugzilla tracking template. You will not see any template links after the edit window. Then click the "show preview" button. You will now see the Bugzilla tracking template linked after the edit window under "view templates".
Comment 17 Timeshifter 2013-06-19 05:54:30 UTC 
Here is a page that has a table and a couple transcluded navigation-box templates at the bottom:
[[List of countries by incarceration rate]]

With VisualEditor (VE) the table is editable and WYSIWYG.

But with VE the existing text in the 2 navigation boxes at the bottom of the page can not be edited as far as I can tell. At least not within VE from that page. I am talking about after clicking the edit icons for the navigation boxes. 

In VE new parameters can be added to one of the templates, but not to the other. I don't see a way to edit the text for existing parameters in either navigation box. 

So to be able to edit one of those templates better in source mode or VE it would be helpful to have links to the template pages. It would be more helpful if those links were more easily accessible than they are now. 

See related bug 49772 - "VisualEditor: Template dialogs should include a link to the [template:name] page". But that is only talking about links to the top-level templates. 

Sometimes I also want links to component templates for various reasons. Such as to be able to read more documentation, choose better templates, etc.. All those templates need to be more accessible by having the template list for a section show up when first clicking the edit link for a section.

Comment 20 Timeshifter 2014-04-30 23:19:18 UTC 
Until I read this thread awhile back I did not know one could get a list of templates used in a section. By any means. So by reading this thread I learned I could click preview to get that list. Funny thing is that I forgot this trick until just now when I read this thread again - after getting an email about this thread being modified. :)

So maybe one thing that could be done to let people know is to add a link called "Show templates in this section". Add it to the top or bottom of the edit window. At least then I would not forget. It could be another preview link, or it could actually do the same thing as a preview but only show the list of templates. Or just the edit window and the list of templates. Preview would be removed or made invisible. 

I am one of the top 2500 editors (by edit total) on Wikipedia, and for years I did not know I could use the "Page information" link in the toolbox to get a list of templates used on the whole page. I always clicked the top edit button on a page to get to templates. Anything that helps editors do things faster is important nowadays. Since there are more articles and less editors. 

The other alternative "parsing the text on edit" would slow down the opening a section for editing, I believe. Never a good idea. Now that I think about it why not avoid showing a list of templates at anytime, even for a whole page, except when one specifically clicks a link: 
*"Show templates in this section". 
*"Show templates in this page". 
That would speed up opening pages and sections for editing. 

That could especially help the visual editor open a page faster for editing. Its long time to open pages with lots of templates and/or references for editing is its fundamental flaw. Especially since VE does not have true section editing.
